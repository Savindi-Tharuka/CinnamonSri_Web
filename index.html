A Smart IoT-integrated system for enhancing Ceylon Cinnamon Production and Export Efficiency.

CinnamonSri empowers smallholder farmers with an end-to-end, mobile-based AI and IoT platform. Our system seamlessly integrates an automated IoT bark drying system, real-time YOLO-based disease detection, intelligent maturity scanning, and AI-driven quality and price prediction, revolutionizing Ceylon Cinnamon production and ensuring fair, efficient, and data-driven cultivation.

Research Domain

Discover how CinnamonSri is transforming the agricultural experience through cutting-edge research.

Research Background

Traditional cinnamon farming in Sri Lanka relies heavily on manual observation and sun drying. This dependence causes inefficiencies, unpredictable quality, and vulnerability to weather, creating a need for accessible, technology-driven solutions for smallholder farmers.

Research Problem

Farmers face inefficiencies across the pipeline: manual disease detection leads to late interventions; traditional drying is weather-dependent and slow; maturity assessment is inconsistent; and pricing relies on inefficient physical market visits and subjective grading.

Methodology

Our approach integrates YOLO and MobileNetV2 for disease and maturity detection, IoT with Raspberry Pi and Edge Impulse for automated drying, and Random Forest for quality and price prediction—all accessible via a React Native app with a Python backend.

Literature Survey

Existing systems are often generalized, lacking crop-specific details like severity-level classification for diseases or dynamic drying models. Previous studies do not combine deep learning with real-time estimations and localized market recommendations tailored for Ceylon Cinnamon.

Research Gap

Most solutions are too complex for small-scale farmers, require heavy cloud dependencies, or fail to provide end-to-end automation. There is no unified system integrating disease detection, maturity scanning, automated drying, and price prediction.

Research Objectives

To develop an integrated, mobile-based AI and IoT platform that automates drying, detects diseases early, classifies stem maturity, and predicts optimal pricing and quality, thereby empowering smallholder farmers and optimizing the cinnamon supply chain.

Key Features

Discover the innovative features that make CinnamonSri exceptional.

Automated IoT Drying System

Real-time temperature control and TinyML dryness detection using edge AI, reducing drying time from days to hours.

AI Disease Detection

YOLO-based deep learning analyzes leaf and bark images to instantly identify diseases, severity levels, and recommend treatments.

Maturity Scanning System

Uses MobileNetV2 and OpenCV to classify cinnamon stem maturity, estimate width, and predict remaining harvest months.

Intelligent Price Prediction

Random Forest models provide accurate grade classification, price estimation, and marketplace recommendations based on simple inputs.

Interactive Farmer App

A comprehensive React Native (Expo) app for real-time monitoring, AI scanning, and intelligent decision support.

Offline-Capable Edge AI

Utilizing Edge Impulse and TinyML to run classification models directly on IoT devices without relying on constant internet access.

Technologies

The complete hardware and software stack powering our smart agriculture platform.

Machine Learning

Random Forest, TinyML, and Edge Impulse for on-device inference and predictive market analytics.

Deep Learning

YOLO, MobileNetV2, and OpenCV for disease detection, maturity classification, and width estimation.

IoT & Hardware

Raspberry Pi, DHT22 sensors, and automated heating systems for localized edge processing and drying.

Mobile Development

React Native and Expo Go for a fast, accessible, cross-platform farmer application.

Backend Core

Python, FlaskAPI, and FastAPI for efficient data routing, image processing, and AI model serving.

Cloud & Database

MongoDB for secure scan history persistence and scalable cloud data storage.
